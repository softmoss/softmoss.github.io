exsucculent!
since these pots are small small I had to search far and low for some saplings that fit, lo and behold these pots are perfect for succulent props! using a couple of broken off leaves from my main succulents I "transplanted" the babies to their newly made pots.

The holes made in the bottom of the pots are perfect for drainage since succulents are notorious for hating wet feet. The added legs to each pot also help overcome that problem. All in all these pots work well with any small plant but cacti and succulents will benefit the most.
