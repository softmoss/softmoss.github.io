me

Belgian based artist with a Masters in animation, mainly in 2D animation but I've got experience in multiple techniques. Currently studying programming backend in C# and finished the javascript language course. While animating and programming are my main interests, I like dipping my hands in al types of crafts. Like embriodery and crocheing my current interest is ceramics. Also looking into riso printing, woodcrafting and screen printing.

Since a young age I've been interested in creating things, Whether it was crafting a simple blog to showcase my sketches in the digital world or growing tomatoes from grocery store seeds in my yard. I've always enjoyed the process of bringing ideas to life.The scratching on the wooden door echoes, he's going to leave marks The act of making things, whether in code or with my hands, has been a journey that continues to shape who I am. It's an ongoing exploration that deepens my understanding of what's achievable in both the digital and physical realms.

When I find a bit of free time you can find me teaching myself how to make games. I've started with unity but since their newest 'update' im looking into Unreal engine and Godot as alternatives.
